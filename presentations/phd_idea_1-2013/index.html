Subjective Measures: Brief Examples

Unexpectedness and Novelty

Represents user background knowledge. Appropriate when:

Subjective knowledge is represented using:

  • Predicate Formuale in the First-Order Logic
  • If-else rules

What are we trying to do differently?

Attempt to mitigate configuration problems in computer science by using interestingness measures to guide the configurations during runtime.

Why?

Because:

  • most configurations are performed before execution
  • have to run multiple times to find optimal configuration
  • configuration may lead to infinite loop
