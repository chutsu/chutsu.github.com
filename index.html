What is Symbolic Regression?

Symbolic Regression (SR) is a type of automatic regression analysis that finds the best model given a dataset, both in terms of accuracy and simplicity.

The difference between SR and conventional regression you may of heard before, is that SR does not assume anything of the data, where as conventional regression seeks to optimize parameters for a pre-specified model, usually defined by a statistian.
